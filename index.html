Japanese Language Courses (JLPT / NAT-Based Program)

Our Japanese Language Program follows the JLPT/NAT framework, providing a structured pathway from beginner to intermediate levels. The course focuses on communication skills, reading, writing, listening, and cultural understanding to prepare students for academic and career opportunities.

JLPT N5 – Beginner Level

Basic Proficiency

  • Hiragana & Katakana writing systems
  • Basic Kanji (~200 characters)
  • Essential vocabulary (~800 words)
  • Basic grammar and sentence formation
  • Everyday greetings & simple conversations
Outcome: Introduce yourself, handle daily conversations, and understand simple Japanese expressions.

JLPT N4 – Elementary Level

  • Additional Kanji (up to ~400 total)
  • Expanded vocabulary (~1,500 words)
  • Intermediate grammar patterns
  • Listening & reading for daily life
  • Expressing opinions and experiences
Outcome: Understand common conversations, read simple passages, and communicate effectively.

JLPT N3 – Intermediate Level

  • Advanced Kanji (up to ~800 total)
  • Expanded vocabulary (~3,700 words)
  • Intermediate to advanced grammar
  • Near-natural speed listening practice
  • Reading articles and structured content
Outcome: Communicate confidently and navigate real-life situations in Japanese environments.
